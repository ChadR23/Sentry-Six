Privacy & Updates

                        
To keep Sentry-Six running smoothly, the app automatically checks for updates and the latest features. We also use a randomly generated ID to see which versions are most popular. This helps us fix bugs faster, and no personal info is ever sent to our servers.

GPS Map Tracking

                        
The floating map displays your vehicle's real-time location and travel path during playback. Event locations are automatically marked on the map. You can drag it anywhere on screen and toggle its visibility in Settings.

                    

                    
                    
                    

                        
Export Your Clips

                        
Set In/Out points, then click Export. Choose cameras, quality, and add telemetry overlay.
