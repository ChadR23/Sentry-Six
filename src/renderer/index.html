Welcome!

                        
Sentry Six is a powerful viewer for Tesla dashcam footage. Let's take a quick tour to help you get started.

                        
                        
                        

                            
                            
                            
Choose your preferred language

                        

                    

                    
                    
                    

                        
Load Your Dashcam Folder

                        
Click "Choose Folder" to load clips. The app will automatically organize your Sentry, SavedClips, and RecentClips.

                        
                        

                            

                                

                                    
                                    
Select your dashcam folder to browse clips

                                    

                                        
                                    

                                

                            

                        

                        
You can select your folder after completing the tour.

                    

                    
                    
                    

                        
Browse Your Clips

                        
The Clips Panel on the left shows all your events. Filter by date, choose an event, and click to play.

GPS Map Tracking

                        
The floating map displays your vehicle's real-time location and travel path during playback. Event locations are automatically marked on the map. You can drag it anywhere on screen and toggle its visibility in Settings.

                    

                    
                    
                    

                        
Export Your Clips

                        
Set In/Out points, then click Export. Choose cameras, quality, and add telemetry overlay.

                        
                        

                            

                                

                                    
                                    
                                    
                                

                            

                        

                    

                    
                    
                    

                        
Customize Settings

                        
Click the gear icon for settings. Configure default folder, shortcuts, metric units, and date format preferences.
